About Us

At Infinite Horizons, we're passionate about unlocking the world's most incredible destinations for our clients.


Established in 2010 by Yadu D Kulkarni, our company is built on a foundation of adventure, expertise, and dedication to delivering unparalleled travel experiences.


With over a decade of experience in the industry, our team has honed its skills in crafting bespoke itineraries that cater to every taste and budget.


Our commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and exceptional customer service is unwavering, ensuring that every journey with Infinite Horizons is nothing short of life-changing.


Let us be your passport to unforgettable adventures – contact us today to start planning your dream trip!
